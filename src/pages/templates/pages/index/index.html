We Are Joining Hacktoberfest 2020

October xth-xth, USTHB, Cyber Espace Register Check Out Our Repo

About

Hacktoberfest is a global monthlong celebration of open source software run by DigitalOcean.

Purpose

By hosting Hacktoberfest 2020 in USTHB, the family of OpenMindsClub strives to encourage everyone to contribute to Free Open-Source projects. We and Digital Ocean are promising awesome prizes to be won and special skills to be learned for the brave ones who are willing to sail our ship so don't waste the opportunity and join us now!

More About the Event

OpenFest is a 2-day event, it will first start with a FOSS Jump and a GIT Workshop on October xxth from xxh to xxh, for the beginners who want to learn more about Open Source and how to contribute to Open Source projects, and will end the second day with the Fest on October xxth from xxh to xxh, where participants will work with each other to contribute to our public Hacktobefest Github repository in the hopes of ending up among the first 50,000 people to complete the challenge!

Everyone Is Welcome

There is no contribution that is either too small or insignificant. However, every pull has to, of course, follow some guidelines. All of OpenMindsClub welcomes you among our family, whether to attend our Talk, Workshop or Complete the challenges. For us, sharing your ideas with this ever growing community is after all, no minor feat! Please read our Events Code of Conduct before attending. Happy Hacking!

Organizer

 

OpenMindsClub is a USTHB scientific club, our goal is to promote collaboration through the mutual sharing of knowledge and the philosophy of Free Open Source among the university students on a smaller scale... and nationally on a larger one.

"That Which Is Not Shared Is Lost," a fundamental ethic which we strive to honor through various events and activities among which is The Install Party, one of our main, dedicated to the gathering of the Open Source community for the traditional GNU/Linux installations, in addition to a collection of conferences, courses, workshops, contests and other activities about Programming, Electronics, Foreign Languages, Photography, Design... and much more, all of which are meticulously prepared by our members throughout the year.

Prizes

In addition to having lots of fun, eating some good snacks, you can earn a limited version goodies if you happen to be among the first 50,000 participants who successfully complete the challenge!

A Really, Really Fun Time!


Throughout the event, you will spend the time gaining hands-on experience with new tools and collaborating with other participants in a welcoming, stress-free, inclusive, fun and collaborative environment. Did we mention that there's food too?

Limited Edition T-Shirt, Stickers and Other Goodies

You will receive a limited edition Hacktoberfest T-shirt, stickers and other Hacktoberfest swag for completing the challenge and submitting 4 valid pull requests.

OpenMindsClub Certification


We will provide signed, digital participation certificates for the Git Workshop as well as the Open Fest!

Get Involved

Everyone is welcome to Hacktoberfest as the participants, so far, have represented 151 countries and thousands of unique skill-sets. Its program welcomes everyone already in the open source software community – and, as a matter of course, anyone interested in diving in!​

To qualify for the official challenge, you must register on DigitalOcean's official website and then make four pull requests between October 1-31. If you want to participate alongside OpenMindsClub, click on the register button on top and wait for our confirmation e-mail to come during the upcoming days!

Of course! We have created many projects that you can contribute to whether you are a programmer or not! But if you want to learn more about Open Source and how to participate, we recommend you join the us on the first day of the event and attend both FOSS Jump and the GIT Workshop.
